Design Rationale

These images were captured from locations symbolizing the development of Oman’s railway infrastructure. Each edit demonstrates different photo-enhancement techniques: object removal to clear distractions, selective color highlight to emphasize engineering elements, and tone adjustments to balance exposure. Filters and HDR processing improved the visual depth while maintaining realism. Responsive image formats (AVIF/WebP with JPEG fallback) ensure optimized loading for all devices, aligning with the module’s technical and aesthetic objectives.
