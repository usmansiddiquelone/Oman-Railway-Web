Design Rationale

The Oman Railway video was edited using CapCut and DaVinci Resolve, focusing on color grading, exposure correction, and animated text transitions. Hardcoded captions were integrated during post-production for accessibility and clarity. Graphic overlays highlight major milestones and project visuals. The video player uses custom JavaScript-based controls to offer play, pause, and seek interactivity, enhancing user engagement. A pop-up overlay introduces key facts midway, and fallback support ensures compatibility with all modern browsers.
