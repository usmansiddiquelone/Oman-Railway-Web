Design Rationale

The Oman Railway sound mix was produced using Audacity and Adobe Audition. Four distinct sources were layered ‚Äî voice narration, railway ambient recordings, metallic sound effects, and a background soundtrack. Editing techniques included fade-in/out for smoother transitions, stereo panning to emulate train motion, and EQ adjustments to balance frequencies. Volume envelopes were automated for dynamic emphasis. The final export was compressed to MP3 for efficient web streaming. The interactive audio player provides playback, mute, and volume control, while a mid-play overlay introduces design insights for a more engaging experience.
